Five Movies You Need to Watch

by Cole Purdy

Suburbia

Follow the lives of angsty, rebellious, apathetic and nihilistic Gen X young adults as a classmate of theirs from high school gomes through town during his massive rock band tour.

He Died With a Falafel in His Hand

A strange vignette film that follows a person through various living situations within his life.

Empire Records

The exact opposite of Suburbia. These kids are idealistic, hopeful and fun. But damn the man. Save the Empire.

Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope

The film that started in all. The Searchers in space.

Management

A weird man who hasn't done anything in his life finds himself infatuated when a weird woman comes to stay at his family's hotel. Impossible not love this movie.
